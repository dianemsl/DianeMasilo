Je m'appelle Diane Masilo, I am currently living in the Netherlands as a graphic design student in ArtEZ, Arnhem.

My projects focus on issues related to human experiences and relationships in today's society, questionning the role of identity/social behaviors through experimental editorial work, and more. I am also open to any kind of visual work!

More about me: I am a movie addict (of course I have a letterboxd account!), I have a sticker collection that is starting to take too much space at my place, I enjoy doing some website coding and i recently started to get a focus interest on tacky, kitsch aesthetic portrait photography
